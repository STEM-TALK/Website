OUR MISSION


STEM TALK is a global organization dedicated to providing traditionally underrepresented students the opportunities that they need to pursue and thrive in STEM careers.

WHAT WE DO


We provide free and comprehensive courses, workshops, and mentorship for students to gain the support and knowledge they need to succeed in STEM, regardless of their personal demographics.

WHO WE ARE


We are an international team of students, leaders, and advocates with a passion for the interdisciplinary fields of STEM and a shared hope that its future can be inclusive and accessible to all.
